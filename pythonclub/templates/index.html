When and Where?

Python Club members meet every Wednesday at 12 noon in Incus (room G10). There is NO minimum bar for entry: a keeness to LEARN, SHARE and HELP others is all we ask for.

Please feel free to turn up on the day. To keep up-to-date with our schedule be sure to add yourself to the Python Club Outlook DL. For technical questions, assistance and python news and discuusion please join the #python-club Slack channel.
